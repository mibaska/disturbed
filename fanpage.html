Disturbed Fan Page

Bio

Disturbed is an alternitive heavy metal band that was formed in 1994 in Chicago, Illinois. Before David Draiman joined the band, they were known as Brawl and their lead singer was Erich Await. The only other former member is bassist Steve Kmak, who was replaced by John Moyer.

Between 2010 and 2015, the band went on hiatus. During that time, Dan Donegan and Mike Wengren became involved with the project Fight or Flight, David Draiman formed the band Device, and John Moyer formed the band Art of Anarchy.

Their studio albums are:

  • The Sickness (2000)
  • Believe (2002)
  • Ten Thousand Fists (2005)
  • Indestructible (2008)
  • Asylum (2010)
  • Immortalized (2015)
  • Evolution (2018)
